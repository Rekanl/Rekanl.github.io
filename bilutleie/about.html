About Us

Lutfi car hire was founded in 1996, by the Lutfi family. Over these two decades, Lutfi car hire has serviced more than 2 million customers in Norway and influenced the landscape of low-cost car rental by bringing technology improvements to customers such as self-checkouts and automated check-ins for safe and contactless rentals. Already active in 2 Norwegian countries in the long-term rental and integrated mobility sector, Lutfi Car hire also provides short and medium-term car rental services with over 6 vehicles. Today, Lutfi car hire is one of the most dynamic companies in the short-term rental sector, which allows for a completely digitised customer journey and contact-free rental.

Location

Our headquarters are based in the heart of Haugesund. We currently operate from over 2 rental branches in Norway, with plans ahead for further expansion. We give great attention to our customer service, handling our reservation and service enquiries.

Vehicles

We operate an impressive fleet in Norway. We aim to cater for every customer need, whether it’s a small car or prestige including BMW or Mercedes. All our cars are in excellent condition and brand new, with an average age of less than 10 years old. We will do everything we can to make our hirers as comfortable as possible and ensure the hire goes smoothly.
